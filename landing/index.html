Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes, completely free forever. Since everything runs locally in your browser, there are no server costs to pass on to you.

How accurate is it?

We use OpenAI's Whisper AI model, the same technology powering many commercial services. Accuracy is on par with industry leaders.

What audio formats are supported?

MP3, WAV, M4A, OGG, and most common audio formats. You can also record directly in the browser.

Does it work offline?

Yes! After the first use (when the AI model downloads), you can transcribe completely offline.

What languages are supported?

Currently English, with multi-language support coming soon. The AI model supports 90+ languages.

Where is my data stored?

Everything is stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB. You can export or delete your data anytime.

Can I self-host it?

Yes! We're open source. You can deploy to your own server or run it completely locally.

What about mobile devices?

Works on mobile browsers, though larger audio files may take longer to process due to device limitations.
